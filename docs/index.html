02 -- How It Works

Every action.
Deterministically verified.

WAND sits between your AI agent and your infrastructure. Every proposed action is evaluated by a deterministic policy engine — no AI, no consensus, no probabilistic reasoning.

Watch
Intercept

Any AI agent — Claude Code, Cursor, AutoGen — proposes an action via MCP or REST. WAND intercepts it before execution.

→
Evaluate
Policy Engine

Deterministic pattern matching against 100+ dangerous patterns. BLOCK, CHALLENGE, or ALLOW. No AI. No confidence scores. No ambiguity.

<500us
Audit
Hash-Chained WAL

Every decision — blocked, challenged, or approved — is recorded with SHA-256(PrevHash + Data). Tamper-evident. SOC2 ready.

Immutable
Never Delegate
Zero AI Decisions

No LLM, no model, no probabilistic system ever decides what is safe. AI can annotate logs after the fact — never approve or block.
